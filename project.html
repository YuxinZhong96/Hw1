Individual work @ ebay Eng. & Research Center (Shanghai) Co. Ltd, 2016.7-2016.9
Exploration in user experience and user interface design

EBAY UNIVERSITY is a website designed for ebay community based in Shanghai to know more about the namesake learning program. The program is aimed to improve them in five fields including leadership, techniques, commerce, general skills and new-employee onboarding. This website is a useful tool for them to select courses, download related course materials, and have online course as well.

DISCOVERY

In order to better understand the project itself, there were conducted interviews among the stakeholder and targeted users.
- Stakeholder interview for main product goals and acceptance and criteria.
- Users interview for general needs using the website and preferred styles.

Through interviews, motivations and frustrations of the users are known, and I also had an understanding of what user want to find in the website.

PROCESS

After the stakeholder and users interview, the function map and the flowchart were accordingly established on the base of brainstorm. They help to
- Define the website and functionalities.
- Develop the relationships among the content and functionalities.

INTERFACE
REFLECTION

Try to be a user rather than merely a designer, which impressed me most during the whole design process of ebay university. As a designer, we need to test our prototypes among our targeted users again and again to make sure everything is perfect.

Try to ask why again and again, and keep getting inspiration from those questions. Whatever we are building, we are constantly making chpices in the request for the better solution. To find out the real reason why a problem happened instead of only treating the symptom.
